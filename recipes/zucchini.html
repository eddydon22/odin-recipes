Zucchini Noodle Casserole

Description

One of my favorite low-calorie dishes that makes me forget about pasta!

Ingredients

  1. olive oil, divided, or as needed
  2. zucchini squash, cut into 'noodles' using a spiral slicer or vegetable peeler
  3. minced garlic
  4. sauce, or to taste
  5. Greek yogurt
  6. pinch Italian seasoning, or to taste
  7. and ground black pepper to taste
  8. shredded mozzarella cheese
